---
title: Non-platform Merchants
---

**Target users**
- B2B collections, ad settlements, international payroll
- Subscriptions, cross-border payments, personal overseas consumption

**Current collection issues**
- Manual collection: poor UX, weak security
- High fees for USD settlement, cumbersome FX conversion

**ePay advantages**
- SaaS global marketplace for merchants without platforms
- Stablecoin cashier for end-user purchases
- Manual invoice QR code generation
- Multi-chain, multi-currency support
- T+1 unlimited fiat withdrawal (USD/EUR/JPY etc.)
