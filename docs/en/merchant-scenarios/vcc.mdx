---
title: VCC Terminal Merchants
---

**Target users**
- Cross-border e-commerce sellers needing overseas procurement
- Agencies/sellers requiring overseas ad spend

**Current collection issues**
- Manual collection: poor real-time experience, weak risk control
- Exchange API: limited currencies, manual USD conversion, extra fees

**ePay advantages**
- Built-in exchange for end-users to buy stablecoins
- Multi-chain, multi-currency acceptance (TRON, ETH, BSC, etc.)
- One-stop global withdrawal, T+1 to banks in USD/EUR/JPY etc.
- Support for corporate and personal bank withdrawals
