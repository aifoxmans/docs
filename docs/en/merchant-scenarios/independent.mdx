**Target users**
- Global online buyers
- Region-specific buyers

**Current collection issues**
- PayPal / credit card: strict risk control, delayed payouts
- Local processors: hard to integrate, limited to local currency

**ePay advantages**
- Easy integration for global stablecoin checkout
- Multi-chain, multi-currency support
- T+1 fiat withdrawal to global banks
